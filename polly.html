Polly is a tortie cat, who is generally very nervous until she gets to know you. Silent for the most part, but when she thinks no-one is watching she finds a spot in the garden and chirps at any birds she can see.

She broke one of her back legs in 2023, and now she has pins in there to keep it together. That doesn't stop her from bounding around at full speed, especially if theres a laser or a shadow involved!
